Community Guidelines

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: We welcome and respect people of all backgrounds and experience levels
  • Learning: We encourage curiosity and continuous learning
  • Collaboration: We believe in working together to achieve common goals
  • Respect: We treat everyone with dignity and respect
  • Integrity: We maintain high ethical standards in all our activities

Guidelines

  1. Be Respectful: Treat all community members with respect and kindness
  2. Be Inclusive: Welcome and support newcomers to the community
  3. Share Knowledge: Contribute to the learning environment by sharing your expertise
  4. Be Professional: Maintain professional conduct in all interactions
  5. Respect Privacy: Protect the privacy of community members
  6. No Harassment: Harassment of any kind will not be tolerated
  7. No Spam: Keep discussions relevant and avoid self-promotion
  8. Follow the Code of Conduct: Adhere to our Code of Conduct

Reporting Concerns

If you experience or witness any behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it to the leadership team immediately. You can contact us through our contact page.
