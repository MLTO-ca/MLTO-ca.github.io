Leadership Team

Mario Gibney is a former English teacher who transitioned to tech after developing interests in bitcoin and AI. Following work at several bitcoin startups, he shifted focus to AI Safety, where he's established numerous community groups. He currently runs Trajectory Labs, a coworking and events space for AI Safety researchers in Toronto, and began collaborating with Myles in 2024 to operate MLTO.
Myles Harrison is a data scientist and consultant turned entrepreneur and company principal of PRAKTIKAI. Previously he was the global lead of the Data Science program at a tech bootcamp and designed and taught his own free and pay-what-you-can online curricula in natural language processing and generative AI. He has been deeply involved with the Toronto tech community for over a decade, as a regular speaker at events, the creator and organizer of the Data Drinks networking group, and now as an organizer of MLTO.
